Melanie Cohen

Melanie is a Program Manager-turned-Dev and happens to be an indecisive hiker. Tired of trying to find hikes on the WTA site only to discover they were too far away or too long for a day trip, she thought a “Find A Hike” app could help her and other PNW hikers discover new adventures that fit their energy and skill levels.
